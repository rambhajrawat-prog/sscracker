Delhi Police – Mirror & Water Image / दर्पण एवं जल प्रतिबिम्ब

🎙 Concept / आधार

Non-Verbal Reasoning में Mirror Image (दर्पण प्रतिबिंब) और Water Image (जल प्रतिबिंब) सबसे आसान और frequently asked topics हैं। Delhi Police में हमेशा basic left-right और up-down reflection पर आधारित questions आते हैं।

✔ Key Rules / मुख्य नियम

Mirror Image (दर्पण प्रतिबिंब)
→ Mirror placed on LEFT or RIGHT
→ Image flips in left-right direction
→ Top–Bottom remains SAME
Water Image (जल प्रतिबिंब)
→ Water surface at BOTTOM
→ Image flips in top-bottom direction
→ Left–Right remains SAME

Tip: बस यह याद रखें — Mirror = Left-Right change, Water = Top-Bottom change.

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

Example 1 – Mirror Image:

Original: A B C → Mirror on right
Mirror Image: C B A
Rule: Reverse horizontally.
Example 2 – Mirror Image:
Original: 3 7 5 Mirror Image: 5 7 3
Example 3 – Water Image:
Original: 1 2 3 Water Image: 3 2 1
Rule: Flip vertically.
Example 4 – Water Image:
Original: ABC Water Image: ABC (just upside-down shape)

✔ Practice MCQs

  1. If mirror is placed on right side of "PQ", mirror image will be?
    Ans: QP
  2. Mirror image of 392?
    Ans: 293
  3. Water image of "UP"?
    Ans: UP (but upside-down orientation)
  4. Mirror image of L-shaped figure faces which direction?
    Ans: Opposite direction (left-right reversed)
  5. Water image of number 86?
    Ans: 86 (top-bottom flipped)
  6. Mirror on left of "N"?
    Ans: N (but horizontally reversed shape)
  7. Mirror image of "E3"?
    Ans: 3E
  8. Water image of 199?
    Ans: 661
  9. Mirror image of "TOY"?
    Ans: YOT
  10. Water image of arrow ↑?
    Ans: ↓

🔥 Challenge Question

Find the mirror image of 2 0 2 5 when mirror is placed on right side.

Reverse horizontally → 5 2 0 2

Motivation: Mirror & Water Image हर साल आता है — 2 मिनट में पूरा chapter strong! 🚓🔥
