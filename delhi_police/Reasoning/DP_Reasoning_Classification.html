Delhi Police – Classification / Odd One Out / वर्गीकरण

🎙 Concept / आधार

Classification का मतलब है चार options में से एक ऐसा ढूँढना जो बाकी तीनों से अलग हो। यह Logic आधारित होता है — meaning, category, number rule, shape rule आदि पर।

Types of Classification / प्रकार

  • Word-based (शब्द)
  • Number-based (संख्या)
  • Alphabet-based (अक्षर)
  • GK-based (सामान्य ज्ञान)
  • Non-verbal figure-based (आकृति आधारित)

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

  1. Dog, Cat, Cow, Tiger
    Dog, Cat, Tiger – carnivores; Cow – herbivore → Odd: Cow
  2. Red, Blue, Green, Apple
    तीन colours; Apple fruit → Odd: Apple
  3. 3, 5, 7, 9
    3,5,7 primes; 9 composite → Odd: 9
  4. A, E, I, K
    A,E,I vowels; K consonant → Odd: K
  5. Car, Bus, Train, Road
    Three vehicles; Road is path → Odd: Road
  6. 12, 18, 24, 35
    Three divisible by 6; 35 not → Odd: 35
  7. Monday, Friday, Sunday, January
    Three days; January month → Odd: January
  8. Pen, Pencil, Eraser, Paper
    Three stationery tools; Paper not a tool → Odd: Paper
