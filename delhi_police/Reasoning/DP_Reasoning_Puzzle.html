Delhi Police – Seating Arrangement & Puzzle / बैठक व्यवस्था व पहेली

🎙 Introduction / परिचय

Delhi Police में Seating Arrangement व Puzzle बहुत आसान स्तर पर पूछे जाते हैं — 2 से 4 statements और simple linear / circular बैठकों पर आधारित।

Types / प्रकार

  • Linear Seating (सीधी लाइन)
  • Circular Seating (वृत्ताकार)
  • Basic Puzzles (age, height, order)
  • Floor Puzzle (2-3 statements)

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Level)

Example 1 – Linear Seating:
A, B, C, D, E sit in a row. C is to the left of D. A is right of B. E is left of B.

One possible arrangement:
E – B – A – C – D
Example 2 – Circular Seating:
A, B, C, D sit around a circle facing centre. A is between B and C. C is to the immediate right of D.

Arrangement:
B – A – C – D
Example 3 – Puzzle (Height):
Among A, B, C – B is taller than A but shorter than C.
So order is:
C > B > A
Example 4 – Floor Puzzle:
Three persons A, B, C live on floors 1 to 3. A does not live on floor 1. C lives above A.

One valid arrangement:
C – A – B (top to bottom)

✔ Practice MCQs

  1. In line A B C D E, if C is between B and D, and A is leftmost:
    Ans: A – B – C – D – E
  2. 4 persons sitting in circle facing centre: A is left of B, C is right of B.
    Ans: B – A – D – C
  3. A > B, C > A, B > D. Who is tallest?
    Ans: C
  4. In linear seating: A is at right end, C is left of A, B is right of D.
    Ans: C – D – B – A
  5. Floor puzzle: A above B, C below B. Top to bottom?
    Ans: A – B – C
  6. Height puzzle: B taller than A, C shorter than A.
    Ans: B > A > C
  7. Circular: Facing centre, A between C and D, B is immediate right of C.
    Ans: C – B – A – D
  8. Linear: D is between A and B, C is rightmost.
    Ans: A – D – B – C
  9. Height: A tallest, C shortest, B between.
    Ans: A > B > C
  10. Floor: B not on 1, A not on 3, C above A.
    Ans: C – A – B

🔥 Challenge Puzzle

A, B, C, D sit in a straight line facing North. B is to the immediate right of C. D is to the left of A. A is to the right of B. What is the order?

Working: C – B – A; D must be left of A → D – C – B – A
