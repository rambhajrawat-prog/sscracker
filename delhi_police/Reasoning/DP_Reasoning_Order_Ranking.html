Delhi Police – Order & Ranking / क्रम व रैंक

🎙 Concept / आधार

Order & Ranking में किसी व्यक्ति/वस्तु की position (स्थान) एक queue, line या group में ज्ञात करनी होती है। Important concepts: Left–Right position, total persons, between count, after-before logic.

✔ Key Formulas / मुख्य सूत्र

  • Total = Left Position + Right Position − 1
  • Middle position = (Total + 1)/2
  • Between = Difference − 1
  • Left–Right conversion: Person count required

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

Example 1:
A is 4th from left and 6th from right. Find total persons.
Total = 4 + 6 − 1 = 9
Example 2:
In a row of 15 persons, A is 7th from left. Position from right?
Right = Total − Left + 1 = 15 − 7 + 1 = 9
Example 3:
A is 10th from left, B is 14th from left. How many between?
Between = 14 − 10 − 1 = 3
Example 4:
A is 5th from right in a group of 20. Position from left?
Left = 20 − 5 + 1 = 16
Example 5:
In a line A is 6th from left and B is 7th from right. Total persons = 12. Find persons between A & B.
Left(B)=Total−Right+1=12−7+1=6 Positions equal → 0 between

🔥 Challenge Question

A is 12th from left, B is 15th from right in a row of 24. How many people are between them?

Left(B) = 24 − 15 + 1 = 10 Difference = |12 − 10| = 2 Between = 2 − 1 = 1
