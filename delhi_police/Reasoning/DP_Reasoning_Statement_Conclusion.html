🎙 Introduction / परिचय

Statement & Conclusion में एक कथन दिया जाता है और पूछा जाता है कि कौन-सा निष्कर्ष तर्कसंगत रूप से follow करता है। Delhi Police में इसके प्रश्न बहुत सरल होते हैं।

✔ Golden Rules / मुख्य नियम

1. केवल वही निष्कर्ष सही है जो statement से सीधा निकलता हो।
2. Extra knowledge, imagination, assumption नहीं जोड़ना है।
3. “Some”, “All”, “No”, “Many”, “Few” जैसे शब्दों को ध्यान से पढ़ें।

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

Example 1:
Statement: “All buses are vehicles.”
Conclusion 1: “All vehicles are buses.”
Conclusion 2: “Some vehicles are buses.”
Answer: Only Conclusion 2 follows
Example 2:
Statement: “No student is lazy.”
Conclusion: “Some students are not lazy.”
Answer: Follows
Example 3:
Statement: “Some apples are red.”
Conclusion: “All apples are red.”
Answer: Does NOT follow
Example 4:
Statement: “All police officers wear uniform.”
Conclusion: “Those who wear uniform may be police officers.”
Answer: Does NOT follow

✔ Practice MCQs (Most Expected)

  1. Statement: “Some boys are tall.” Conclusion: “Some tall persons are boys.” Ans: Follows
  2. Statement: “All cars are machines.” Conclusion: “Some machines are cars.” Ans: Follows
  3. Statement: “No dog is a cat.” Conclusion: “Some cats are not dogs.” Ans: Follows
  4. Statement: “Some teachers are women.” Conclusion: “All women are teachers.” Ans: Does not follow
  5. Statement: “All mobiles are electronic items.” Conclusion: “Some electronic items are mobiles.” Ans: Follows
  6. Statement: “Many students passed the exam.” Conclusion: “Some students passed the exam.” Ans: Follows
  7. Statement: “Some fruits are sweet.” Conclusion: “All sweet things are fruits.” Ans: Does not follow
  8. Statement: “No bird is animal.” Conclusion: “All birds are animals.” Ans: Does not follow
  9. Statement: “All officers are graduates.” Conclusion: “Some graduates are officers.” Ans: Follows
  10. Statement: “Some books are costly.” Conclusion: “Some costly things are books.” Ans: Follows

🔥 Challenge Question

Statement: “All Delhi Police constables are brave.”
Conclusion: “Some brave people are Delhi Police constables.”

Answer: Follows ✔
