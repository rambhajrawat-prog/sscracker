Delhi Police – Direction Sense / दिशा ज्ञान

🎙 Concept / आधार

Direction Sense में व्यक्ति की चाल (movement) और दिशा (East, West, North, South) के आधार पर final position और distance पता करना होता है।

Important Directions:

  • North (उत्तर)
  • South (दक्षिण)
  • East (पूरब)
  • West (पश्चिम)
  • NE, NW, SE, SW (diagonals)

✔ Key Rules / महत्वपूर्ण नियम

  • East → Right side from North
  • West → Left side from North
  • North-East = 45° between North & East
  • Distance = Pythagoras when right-angle turn
  • Always draw diagram lightly for accuracy

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

Example 1:
A walks 5 km North, then 3 km East. How far is A from starting point?

Right angle triangle → √(5² + 3²) = √34 = 5.8 km approx
Example 2:
A walks 4 km South, then 4 km West. Direction from start? → South-West direction Distance = √(4²+4²)= 5.6 km
Example 3:
A walks 10 km East, then 10 km North, then 10 km West. Final position? → 10 km North of starting point Distance = 10 km
Example 4:
A walks 6 km North, then 2 km South. Final distance from start = 6−2 = 4 km North

    Ans: North-East

🔥 Challenge Question

A walks 6 km East, 8 km North, 6 km West, 2 km South. Find final distance from starting point.

Net horizontal: 6−6 = 0
Net vertical: 8−2 = 6 km
Answer: 6 km North
