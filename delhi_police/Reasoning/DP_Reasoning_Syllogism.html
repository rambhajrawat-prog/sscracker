Delhi Police – Syllogism / निगमन

🎙 Concept / आधार

Syllogism में दो या अधिक statements दिए जाते हैं और पूछा जाता है कि कौन-सा conclusion 100% true, false या doubtful है। Delhi Police exam में traditional Venn diagram method आता है — कोई tricky pattern नहीं।

✔ Standard Statements / मानक कथन

1. All A are B → A पूरी तरह B में शामिल
2. Some A are B → कुछ A, B हैं
3. No A is B → A और B अलग-अलग हैं
4. Some A are not B → कुछ A, B नहीं हैं

Always use Venn Diagram → safest and exam-perfect method.

✔ Solved Examples (Delhi Police Pattern)

Example 1:
Statements:
1. All cats are animals.
2. Some animals are wild.

Conclusion:
I. Some cats are wild.
II. All animals are cats.

Solution:
I → Not definite (Cats may or may not be wild) → False
II → Impossible → False
Correct Answer: Neither I nor II
Example 2:
Statements:
1. Some dogs are pets.
2. All pets are loyal.

Conclusions:
I. Some dogs are loyal.
II. All dogs are loyal.

Solution:
I → True (Dogs → Pets → Loyal)
II → Not definite
Correct Answer: Only I follows
Example 3:
Statements:
1. All apples are fruits.
2. No fruit is vegetable.

Conclusions:
I. No apple is vegetable.
II. Some vegetables are not apples.

Solution:
I → True (Apple ⟹ Fruit ⟹ No veg)
II → True (Vegetables are outside fruits completely)
Correct Answer: Both I and II follow

✔ Practice MCQs

  1. Statements: All books are papers. Some papers are files. Conclusion: I. Some books are files. II. All files are books. Ans: Neither I nor II
  2. Statements: Some cars are buses. All buses are vehicles. Conclusion: I. Some cars are vehicles. II. All vehicles are buses. Ans: Only I follows
  3. Statements: All roses are flowers. Some flowers are red. Conclusion: I. Some roses are red. II. All red are roses. Ans: Neither I nor II
  4. Statements: No A is B. Some B are C. Conclusion: I. Some C are not A. II. All C are A. Ans: Only I follows
  5. Statements: All students are intelligent. No intelligent is lazy. Conclusion: I. No student is lazy. II. Some lazy are not students. Ans: Both I and II follow
  6. Statements: Some fruits are sweet. All sweet things are good. Conclusion: I. Some fruits are good. II. All good things are fruits. Ans: Only I follows
  7. Statements: Some A are B. Some B are C. Conclusion: I. Some A are C. II. No A is C. Ans: Neither I nor II
  8. Statements: All pens are tools. Some tools are sharp. Conclusion: I. Some pens are sharp. II. No pen is sharp. Ans: Neither I nor II
  9. Statements: No bird is animal. All animals are living beings. Conclusion: I. No bird is living being. II. Some living beings are not birds. Ans: Only II follows
  10. Statements: All chairs are wooden. Some wooden things are heavy. Conclusion: I. Some chairs are heavy. II. All heavy things are wooden. Ans: Neither I nor II

🔥 Challenge Question

Statements:
1. All A are B.
2. Some B are C.
Conclusion:
I. Some A are C.
II. All C are A.

I = Not definite II = Impossible Answer: Neither I nor II follows

Motivation: Syllogism में केवल एक rule — जो 100% guaranteed नहीं है, वह “Follow” नहीं करता। यही Delhi Police का scoring shortcut है। 🚓🔥
