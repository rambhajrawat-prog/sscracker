Delhi Police – Chapter 13: Mensuration 3D / त्रि–आयामी क्षेत्रमिति

🎙 Teacher-Style Introduction

Mensuration 3D में हम वस्तुओं के कुल पृष्ठीय क्षेत्रफल (Total Surface Area – TSA), वक्र पृष्ठीय क्षेत्रफल (Curved Surface Area – CSA) और आयतन (Volume) सीखते हैं। Delhi Police Exam में 1–2 आसान सवाल आते हैं, mostly cuboid, cylinder और sphere से।

📘 Important Formulas / मुख्य सूत्र

Solid Shape CSA TSA Volume
Cuboid (घनाभ) — 2(lb + bh + lh) l × b × h
Cube (घन) — 6a² a³
Cylinder (बेलन) 2πrh 2πr(r + h) πr²h
Cone (शंकु) πrl πr(r + l) ⅓ πr²h
Sphere (गोला) — 4πr² 4/3 πr³
Hemisphere (अर्द्धगोला) 2πr² 3πr² 2/3 πr³

⚡ Tricks & Exam Shortcuts

  • Cylinder & cube are most common in DP exams.
  • CSA = curved (side) area; TSA = total (includes top & bottom).
  • Volume always in cubic units (cm³, m³).
  • Always note radius vs diameter — DP exam में trick यही है।
  • Slope length (l) in cone → l = √(r² + h²).

✔ Solved Examples / हल प्रश्न

Example 1: Cuboid: l=6, b=4, h=3 TSA = 2(lb + lh + bh) = 2(24 + 18 + 12) = 108

Example 2: Cube: side=5 TSA = 6a² = 150 Volume = 125

Example 3: Cylinder: r=7, h=10 CSA = 2πrh = 2×22/7×7×10 = 440 Volume = πr²h = 22/7×49×10 = 1540

Example 4: Cone: r=7, h=24 l = √(7²+24²)=25 CSA = πrl = 22/7×7×25 = 550

Example 5: Sphere: r=7 TSA = 4πr² = 4×22/7×49 = 616

Example 6: Hemisphere r=3 TSA = 3πr² = 3×3×3×22/7 = 84.85 approx

Example 7: Cylinder diameter=14 (radius=7), height=5 TSA = 2πr(r+h)=2×22/7×7×12 = 528

Example 8: Cube edge=4 → diagonal = a√3 = 4√3

🔥 Challenge Question

A closed cylindrical tank has radius 10 cm and height 20 cm. Find total surface area and volume.

TSA = 2πr(r+h) = 2×22/7×10×30 = 1885.7 cm²

Volume = πr²h = 22/7×100×20 = 6285.7 cm³
