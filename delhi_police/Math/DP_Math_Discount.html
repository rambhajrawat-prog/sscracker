Delhi Police – Chapter 6: Discount / छूट

🎙 Teacher-Style Introduction

Discount chapter बिलकुल real-life market based topic है — MRP, Selling Price, Discount %, Successive Discount. Delhi Police Constable exam में यह हमेशा 2 आसान questions देता है — fast calculation required.

📘 Important Formulas

  • Discount = Marked Price − Selling Price
  • Discount% = (Discount / Marked Price) × 100
  • Selling Price = MP × (100 − D)% / 100
  • Successive Discount Formula: Final Discount = A + B − (A×B)/100
  • छूट = अंकित मूल्य − विक्रय मूल्य
  • छूट% = (छूट / अंकित मूल्य) × 100
  • विक्रय मूल्य = MP × (100 − छूट%) / 100
  • क्रमिक छूट: Final Discount = A + B − (A×B)/100

⚡ Tricks & Shortcuts

  • Discount हमेशा Marked Price पर निकाला जाता है.
  • Successive discount “Add then subtract product/100”.
  • DP exam में mostly 1 discount या 2 successive discount आते हैं.

✔ Solved Examples

Example 1: MP = 500, Discount = 10%. SP = ?
SP = 500 × 90/100 = ₹450.

Example 2: MP = 800, SP = 680. Find discount%.
Discount = 800−680 = 120 → (120/800)×100 = 15%.

Example 3 (Successive): Discounts = 10% and 20%.
Final = 10 + 20 − (200/100) = 28%.

Example 4: MP = 600, discount% = 25%. SP = ?
SP = 600 × 75/100 = ₹450.

Example 5: A shopkeeper gives 12% discount on MP 400. SP = ?
SP = 400×0.88 = ₹352.

Example 6: MP = 1000, discount = 30% then 20%.
Final discount = 30 + 20 − 6 = 44%.

Example 7: Find discount%: MP = 300, SP = 255.
Discount = 45 → % = 45/300×100 = 15%.

Example 8: 2 successive discounts of 15% each.
Final = 15+15−2.25 = 27.75%.

🔥 Challenge Question

Final discount = 20 + 10 − 2 = 28% SP = 2000 × 0.72 = ₹1440.
