Delhi Police – Chapter 11: Average / औसत

🎙 Teacher-Style Introduction

Average chapter बहुत scoring और आसान होता है। Delhi Police exam में हर साल 1–2 direct सवाल आते हैं — बस total निकालो और संख्या से divide कर दो।

📘 Important Formulas

  • Average = (Sum of items) / (Number of items)
  • Total = Average × Number
  • New Average Method (जल्दी हल करने का तरीका)
  • Equal increase/decrease → average भी उतना ही बदलता है

⚡ Tricks

  • “Equal change method” सबसे तेज़
  • Missing number निकालने का fastest तरीका → Total difference method

✔ Examples

Example 1: Numbers: 4, 6, 8 Average = (4+6+8)/3 = 6

Example 2: Avg = 20, items = 6 → Total = 120

Example 3: Five numbers avg = 10. If sixth is 20 → New avg?
Total old=50, new total = 70 → avg = 70/6 = 11.67

Example 4: Avg of 5 numbers is 18. One number is 28.
Remaining total = 90−28 = 62 → avg = 15.5

Example 5: If a number 10 is removed from avg 15 of 6 numbers:
Total old=90, new total=80 → new avg=80/5= 16
