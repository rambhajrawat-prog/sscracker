Delhi Police – Chapter 12: Mensuration 2D / क्षेत्रमिति 2D

🎙 Teacher-Style Introduction

Mensuration 2D chapter में हम उन आकृतियों के क्षेत्रफल (Area) और परिमाप (Perimeter) सीखते हैं जो रोज़मर्रा के जीवन में दिखती हैं—Square, Rectangle, Triangle, Circle आदि। Delhi Police Exam में **2–3 direct formula-based** सवाल आते हैं। यदि formula याद है → 10 seconds में answer।

📘 Important Formulas / महत्वपूर्ण सूत्र

ShapeAreaPerimeter
Square (वर्ग) a² 4a
Rectangle (आयत) L×B 2(L+B)
Triangle (त्रिभुज) ½ × base × height a+b+c
Right Triangle ½ × perpendicular × base Pythagoras: h² = p² + b²
Circle (वृत्त) πr² 2πr
Parallelogram (समांतर चतुर्भुज) b × h 2(a+b)
Trapezium (समलम्ब) ½ (a + b)h a+b+c+d
Rhombus (समचतुर्भुज) ½ (d₁ × d₂) 4a

⚡ Tricks & Shortcuts

  • Circle के सवाल सबसे ज्यादा आते हैं → π = 22/7 use करें।
  • Square/Rectangle → diagonal = √(L² + B²)
  • Trapezium → हमेशा height दी होती है, confuse मत होइए।
  • Triangle का Heron's Formula rarely आता है (though simple).

✔ Solved Examples / हल प्रश्न

Example 1: Side of square = 12 cm. Area = 12² = 144 cm²

Example 2: Rectangle: L=15, B=10 Area = 150 Perimeter = 50

Example 3: Circle radius = 7 Area = 22/7 × 49 = 154

Example 4: Triangle: base=20, height=8 Area = ½×20×8 = 80

Example 5: Parallelogram: base=10, height=6 Area = 60

Example 6: Rhombus diagonals = 10, 12 Area = ½×10×12 = 60

Example 7: Trapezium: parallel sides 10, 16, height 8 Area = ½(10+16)×8 = 104

Example 8: Circle: diameter=14 → radius=7 Perimeter = 2πr = 44

✔ MCQs (Delhi Police Pattern)

  1. Square side=10 → area? 100
  2. Circle r=14 → perimeter? 88
  3. Rectangle L=12,B=8 → area? 96
  4. Triangle b=10,h=12 → area? 60
  5. Parallelogram base=9,h=7 → area? 63
  6. Circle diameter=10 → area? 78.5 approx
  7. Trapezium sides 8,12 and h=6 → area= 60
  8. Rhombus d1=8,d2=6 → area= 24
  9. Square diagonal=14 → side= 9.9 approx
  10. Circle perimeter=44 → radius? 7

🔥 Challenge Question

A circular garden has radius 21 m. Find area & fencing cost if fencing is ₹20 per meter.

Area = πr² = 22/7 × 441 = 1386 m²
Perimeter = 2πr = 2 × 22/7 × 21 = 132 m
Fencing cost = 132 × 20 = ₹2640
