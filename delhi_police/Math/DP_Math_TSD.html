Delhi Police – Chapter 10: Time, Speed & Distance (TSD) / समय, गति व दूरी

🎙 Teacher-Style Introduction

Time, Speed & Distance वह chapter है जहाँ concept सिर्फ एक है — Distance = Speed × Time. Delhi Police exam में 2–3 सवाल direct इसी formula पर आधारित आते हैं।

Train, car, walking speed — सारे questions आसान और calculation-friendly होते हैं।

📘 Important Formulas / महत्वपूर्ण सूत्र

  • Distance = Speed × Time
  • Speed = Distance / Time
  • Time = Distance / Speed
  • 1 km/hr = 5/18 m/s
  • 1 m/s = 18/5 km/hr
  • Relative Speed (Same direction) = A − B
  • Relative Speed (Opp direction) = A + B
  • दूरी = गति × समय
  • गति = दूरी / समय
  • समय = दूरी / गति
  • 1 किमी/घंटा = 5/18 मी/से
  • विपरीत दिशा = गति जोड़ें
  • एक ही दिशा = गति घटाएँ

⚡ Tricks & Shortcuts

  • Unit conversion हमेशा check करें (km/hr ↔ m/s).
  • Relative speed वाले questions fastest होते हैं (2-step).
  • DP exam में tough train questions नहीं आते — सिर्फ basic TSD.
  • “Speed doubled → time half” type relations याद रखें।

✔ Solved Examples / हल प्रश्न

Example 1: Speed = 40 km/hr, Time = 2 hours. Find distance.
→ D = 40×2 = 80 km

Example 2: D = 150 km, S = 50 km/hr → Time?
→ T = 150/50 = 3 hr

Example 3: Convert 54 km/hr into m/s.
→ 54 × 5/18 = 15 m/s

Example 4: Convert 10 m/s into km/hr.
→ 10 × 18/5 = 36 km/hr

Example 5: Two cars at 40 & 30 km/hr opposite direction.
→ Relative speed = 70 km/hr

Example 6: Two persons walk at 5 km/hr & 3 km/hr same direction.
→ Relative = 2 km/hr

Example 7: A car covers 120 km in 3 hours. Find speed.
→ S = 120/3 = 40 km/hr

Example 8: A man increases speed from 10 to 20 km/hr. Time becomes?
→ Time becomes half.

✔ MCQs (Delhi Police Pattern)

  1. Speed = 60 km/hr, Time = 2 hr. Distance? Ans: 120 km
  2. Distance = 90 km, Speed = 30 km/hr. Time? Ans: 3 hr
  3. Convert 36 km/hr to m/s. Ans: 10 m/s
  4. Convert 20 m/s to km/hr. Ans: 72 km/hr
  5. Opp direction: 45 & 55 km/hr. Relative speed? Ans: 100 km/hr
  6. Same direction: 50 & 30 km/hr. Relative speed? Ans: 20 km/hr
  7. Time = 4 hr, Distance = 200 km. Speed? Ans: 50 km/hr
  8. A man walks 4 km/hr, runs 8 km/hr. Ratio of times? Ans: 2 : 1
  9. A covers 150 km in 5 hours → speed? Ans: 30 km/hr
  10. If speed × time = 180, distance = ? Ans: 180

🔥 Challenge Question

A bus travels at 48 km/hr. How much time will it take to cover 40 km?

Time = Distance / Speed = 40/48 = 0.833 hr = 50 minutes.

💡 Motivation: Speed important है, पर direction उससे भी ज्यादा — exam में भी और जिंदगी में भी! 🚓🔥
