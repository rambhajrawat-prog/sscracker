Computer Based Examination – Overview

  • Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Total Questions: 100
  • Total Marks: 100
  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Negative Marking: As per latest official notification.

Section-wise Distribution

  • GK / Current Affairs – 25 Q (25 Marks)
  • Reasoning – 25 Q (25 Marks)
  • Numerical Ability (Maths) – 25 Q (25 Marks)
  • Computer Awareness – 25 Q (25 Marks)

Key Topics (Brief)

Maths

Number System, Simplification, Percentage, Profit & Loss, SI & CI (basic), Ratio & Proportion, Average, Time & Work, Time–Speed–Distance, Mensuration, Mixture & Alligation, DI.

Reasoning

Analogy, Classification, Series, Coding-Decoding, Blood Relation, Direction, Order & Ranking, Venn Diagram, Syllogism (basic), Non-verbal.

GK / GA

History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, Sports, Awards, Books & Authors, Important Days, National & International Events, Delhi-specific GK.

Computer

Fundamentals, Hardware/Software, MS Office, Internet & Email, Shortcut keys, Basic security.
